# 📊 INX Future Inc Employee Performance Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **INX Future Inc Employee Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary hikes, education backgrounds, and job role performance. The dashboard provides HR managers and business leaders with actionable insights to improve employee retention and workforce planning.


---

## 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric                   | Value     |
| ------------------------ | --------- |
| Total Employees          | 1200      |
| Attrition Count          | 178       |
| Attrition Rate           | 15%       |
| Average Employee Age     | 37 Years  |
| Average Salary Hike      | 15%       |
| Average Years at Company | 7.1 Years |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Employee Overview

* Total employee count
* Average employee age
* Average years of service
* Average salary hike percentage

### 2️⃣ Attrition Analysis

* Overall attrition count and rate
* Gender-wise attrition comparison
* Age group attrition analysis
* Department-level filtering

### 3️⃣ Education Analysis

Employee attrition categorized by educational background:

* Life Sciences – 37%
* Medical – 25%
* Marketing – 17%
* Technical Degree – 13%
* Other – 4%

### 4️⃣ Job Role Analysis

Tracks employee attrition across different job roles:

* Sales Executive
* Developer
* Sales Representative
* Research Scientist
* Manager R&D
* Human Resources
* Laboratory Technician
* Senior Developer

### 5️⃣ Years at Company Analysis

Visualizes attrition trends based on employee tenure, helping identify periods with higher turnover.

### 6️⃣ Salary Hike Impact

Analyzes the relationship between salary hike percentages and employee attrition.
